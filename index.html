A freelance analytics dashboard built for a real estate manager to keep track of her Facebook lead inquiries in real time. The dashboard features automated data pulling directly from her Facebook page, eliminating manual data entry and ensuring her pipeline is always up to date.

Key metrics include total leads, active campaigns, conversion rate, pipeline velocity, and new leads per day.
